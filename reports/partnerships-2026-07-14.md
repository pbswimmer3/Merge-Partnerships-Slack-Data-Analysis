## Executive Summary

- **477** messages from **48** unique users, including **94** questions across **53** threads.
- **69** unanswered questions; median first-reply latency: **8.2m**.

Over the roughly three-month period, this channel saw 477 messages and 94 questions from 48 unique users across 53 threads, with activity noticeably intensifying in late June and July (peaks of 34–35 messages on 6/24 and 7/10). Excluding the large "other" bucket (64), the most common technical question categories were API/technical and integration/connector issues (10 each), followed by bug reports (4) and pricing/commercial inquiries (3). The most pressing concern is responsiveness: 69 of 94 questions went unanswered, and the median first-reply latency was about 8 minutes, suggesting that while quick replies happen when they do occur, a large share of questions slip through entirely. This gap points to a strong automation opportunity, such as an auto-triage bot that routes API and integration questions to the right owners and flags unanswered threads after a set interval. Building a knowledge-base or FAQ auto-responder for the recurring API and connector topics could also deflect a meaningful portion of repetitive queries and improve answer coverage.
