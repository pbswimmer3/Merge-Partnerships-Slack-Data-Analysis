## Executive Summary

- **477** messages from **48** unique users, including **94** questions across **53** threads.
- **69** unanswered questions; median first-reply latency: **8.2m**.

Over the roughly three-month window, the channel saw 477 messages and 94 questions from 48 unique users across 53 threads, with activity clearly ramping up in late June and July (peak days of 34-35 messages on 06/24 and 07/10). Excluding the large "other" bucket, the most common question categories were API/technical and integration/connector issues (10 each), followed by bug reports (4) and pricing/commercial inquiries (3), pointing to a technically-oriented partner audience. The most concerning trend is responsiveness: 69 of 94 questions (about 73%) went unanswered, and the median first-reply latency was roughly 8 minutes for those that did get a reply. This large unanswered gap suggests coverage or triage breakdowns rather than slow replies, since answered questions were handled quickly. Given the concentration in API/technical and integration topics, there is a strong automation opportunity to deploy a bot or knowledge-base auto-responder for common technical and connector questions, plus an unanswered-question alerting workflow to reduce the backlog.
